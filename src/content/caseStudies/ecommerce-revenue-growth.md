# StyleHouse E-commerce: $2.4M Revenue Growth

## The Challenge

StyleHouse, a direct-to-consumer fashion brand, had hit a growth plateau. After years of strong performance, their marketing efficiency had declined significantly:

- ROAS dropped from 4.5x to 2.1x
- Email revenue contribution was under 10%
- Customer acquisition costs were rising
- Repeat purchase rate had stagnated

## Our Approach

### Email Automation Revival

We completely rebuilt their email program:

1. **Segmentation overhaul**: Created 12 distinct customer segments
2. **Welcome series**: Achieved 52% open rate, 8% conversion
3. **Browse abandonment**: Recovered 15% of lost sessions
4. **Post-purchase flow**: Increased repeat purchase rate by 40%
5. **Win-back campaigns**: Reactivated 8% of lapsed customers

### Paid Media Restructuring

Complete account restructure across platforms:

- **Google Shopping**: Implemented Smart Shopping with product segmentation
- **Meta Ads**: Launched Advantage+ campaigns with creative testing
- **Retargeting**: Built full-funnel retargeting across platforms
- **Budget allocation**: Shifted to highest-ROAS channels

### Analytics Implementation

Built comprehensive tracking:

- Enhanced ecommerce tracking in GA4
- Custom attribution model implementation
- Customer lifetime value tracking
- Cohort analysis dashboards

## Results

The transformation was significant:

- Annual revenue increased by $2.4M
- Email revenue grew 180% in 6 months
- ROAS improved from 2.1x to 4.2x
- Customer lifetime value increased 65%

## Key Takeaways

1. **Email is an untapped channel**: Many brands underinvest in email automation
2. **ROAS isn't everything**: Focusing on LTV changed budget allocation decisions
3. **Creative matters**: Testing creative drove 40% of performance improvement
4. **Integration amplifies**: Email + paid media created compounding returns
